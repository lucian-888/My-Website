Guinguette de Kercabiron

Brittany, France · Ongoing

Guinguette de Kercabiron is a long-term documentary project following the life of a seasonal community gathering place in Brittany. Through volunteer work, local markets and seasonal gatherings, the project observes how people collectively create, inhabit and transform this shared place over time.
