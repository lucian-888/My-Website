About

Documentary Photographer
Based in Brittany, France

I am a documentary photographer based in Brittany, France. My work is built through long-term observation, returning to the same places over months and years, allowing visual narratives to emerge slowly from everyday life.

Originally from Romania, I lived in the United States from 2004 to 2011 before settling in France in 2012. Today my work moves between France and Romania, exploring questions of community, memory, belonging, and migration.

Rather than documenting isolated events, I am interested in places that evolve over time. By returning repeatedly, I seek to understand how relationships, routines, and collective spaces gradually reveal themselves through photography.

I photograph places I intend to return to.
